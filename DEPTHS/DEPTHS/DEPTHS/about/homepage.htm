Bio Sketch

Zoran Jeremic was born in 1974, in Kraljevo, Serbia and Montenegro. He has graduated at the Military Academy in Belgrade. He received the M.S. degrees in informatics and computer engineering from the Department of Computer Science, FON - School of Business Administration, University of Belgrade, Serbia and Montenegro. He is currently an PhD student at the Department of Information Systems, FON - School of Business Administration, University of Belgrade, Serbia and Montenegro. His current research interests are in the area of Intelligent tutoring system, student modeling, XML-based learning metadata sharing, and Web-based educational systems. So far, he has authore/co-authored several reseach papers. Mr. Jeremic is a member of the GOOD OLD AI research group
